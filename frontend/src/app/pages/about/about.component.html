About Us

At Sous & You, we aim to solve the issue of “What should I cook?” when a user has time restrictions, preferences, dietary restrictions, cooking skill, tools, and specific ingredients or a budget they need to follow. Based on each user’s profile and inputs, the AI Recipe Recommender will provide the recipes as well as personalized tips and cooking recommendations for each recipe.
